Computer First Generation :-

The first generation computer used vacuume tube as main electronic component. Due to high cost of vacuume tube, less costly but slower devices mercury delay line was used as a memory. Machine dependant low level language was used for programing.

The importent features first generation computer are :-

  • Vacuume tube was used as main electronic component.
  • Processing speed was mini-speed.
  • They use machine dependant language for programing
  • Larger amount of heat was generated.
  • Required A.C.
  • Power consumption was very large.
  • Not Reliable
  • Very large in size
  • Not portable

Example of first generation of computer are :-

  1. ENIAC
  2. EDVAC
  3. UNIVAC
