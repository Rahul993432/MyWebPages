Computer Third Generation :-

In which generation integrated circuit( SSI and MSI ) was used in CPU, higher processer and other electronic component.
The user of integrated circuit made the computer smaller, faster and more energy efficiant.

The importent features Third generation computer are :-

  • Integrated circuit was used CPU, higher processor and other electronic component.
  • The processing speed was increased to nano speed.
  • Semi conductor was used as a main memory instit of magnetic core.
  • Machine indipendent high level language was use.
  • Parallel processing and multi programing were developed.
  • Computer were smaller, faster, relibule and more energy efficiant.
